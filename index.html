ABOUT ME

All my life I have been a creative. It started with a love for drawing and I was sure I wanted to become a concept artist, since I have a deep love for gaming and most things geeky. However drawing was something I enjoyed, but was not amazing at and lets be honest, the competition to work for Blizzard is next level. After highschool I was really unsure of what I wanted to do. I thought about becoming a teacher or doing interior decorating, but somehow ended up on a enrollment form for graphic design. Long story short, I liked the idea, erolled and there I was...having absolutely no idea what I was doing. It took me a while to realize that art and design were not the same thing, but slowly I got the hang of it and fell completely and madly inlove with every little thing about it. I wanted to further indulge myself in the world of design, so after my print and publishing course, I went to study web design and oh boy...first came tears and now coding is life. I still have a lot to learn and absolutely crave to do so. So let us work together, there are perks: I know how to make things pretty on a computer and sometimes im funny.
